Bó Hub

Simple livestock management for Irish farmers

Manage your herd with ease

Bó Hub helps Irish cattle farmers keep on top of the herd — all on your phone, all in one place.

Herd & records

  • Keep records for every animal: tag, breed, date of birth, movements
  • Scan animals with a Bluetooth EID tag reader for instant lookup
  • Build your own groupings and alerts (SmartFilters) for your herd
  • Excel import and export of your herd

Health & compliance

  • Track TB tests and 30- & 36-month age limits
  • Manage treatments and your drug cabinet
  • See at a glance which animals are still in meat or milk withdrawal
  • My Farm Alerts — get notified of important vaccine dates and herd events
  • Bord Bia audit pack — remedy, purchase, movement and mortality registers exported in one go
  • Nitrates calculator — check your stocking rate against the limits
  • Log contractor jobs (slurry, spraying, silage) with costs and spray records
  • Keep track of TAMS grant schemes and their important dates

Prices & money

  • Live Irish cattle market prices and trends
  • Value your cattle with the beef price calculator, using live factory prices

Your data stays private on your own phone — no accounts, no tracking — with full backup and restore so it's never lost.

Try it straight away — the app comes with demo cattle already loaded, so you can fully evaluate every feature. Or load your own animals: export your herd from AIM in Excel format and import that file into the app (Settings → Import Excel) — until the app talks directly to the Department of Agriculture (AIM).
