Bó Hub

Simple livestock management for Irish farmers

Manage your herd with ease

Bó Hub helps Irish cattle farmers keep on top of the herd — all on your phone, all in one place.

  • Keep records for every animal: tag, breed, date of birth, movements
  • Track TB tests and 30- & 36-month age limits
  • Manage treatments and your drug cabinet
  • Build your own groupings and alerts (smart lists) for your herd
  • Bord Bia audit support and Excel import/export
  • Live Irish cattle market prices and trends

Your data stays private on your own phone — no accounts, no tracking.

This app is offline. Bó Hub does not connect to the Department of Agriculture (AIM) website. To load your animals, export your herd from AIM in Excel format and import that file into the app (Settings → Import Excel).
