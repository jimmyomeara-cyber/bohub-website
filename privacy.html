Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Bó Hub (“the app”) is a livestock-management app for Irish farmers, provided by Ballycrine Technologies. This policy explains how the app handles your information. In short: your farm data stays on your phone, and we do not collect it.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information or farm data. Bó Hub has no user accounts and no central server run by us.

2. Where your data is stored

Everything you enter — animal records, herd details, treatments, and your profile — is stored only on your own device. It is not sent to us or to anyone else. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, that information is permanently deleted.

3. Internet connection

The app uses the internet for one purpose only: to show the latest Irish cattle market prices, which it retrieves from the European Commission’s public agricultural data service. No personal or farm data is sent when it does this.

4. No tracking or advertising

Bó Hub contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

5. Sharing your data

Because we never collect your data, we have nothing to share or sell. Any exporting of your records (for example to a spreadsheet or PDF) is carried out by you, on your device, entirely under your control.

6. Children

The app is intended for farmers and is not directed at children.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date shown above.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at info@bohub.ie.
